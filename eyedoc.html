Meet Our Specialist

Dr. Vatsala Nidhi

Ophthalmologist

Dr. Vatsala Nidhi's journey in medicine commenced with a remarkable achievement, securing the 18th All India Rank in her AIPMT examinations. She earned her MBBS degree from the esteemed Maulana Azad Medical College, renowned for its academic excellence. Continuing her pursuit of excellence. During her tenure at AIIMS, Dr. Nidhi meticulously honed her skills across various ophthalmic specialties, encompassing cataract surgery, comprehensive eye examination, and the evaluation and management of diverse conditions such as glaucoma, retina disorders, oculoplasty, and traumatic eye injuries. Furthermore, she sought additional training in Cornea, Ocular Surface, and Refractive Surgery services.

Achievements:

  • Dr. Nidhi pursued elective hands-on training in the department of Rheumatology at the University of Alabama in the USA, further enriching her medical acumen with international exposure and experience.
  • Cleared her DNB and Fellowship in International Council of Ophthalmology examinations and has numerous publications to her credit.
  • Worked at Dr. Shroff's Charity Eye Hospital, New Delhi, for two years.

Qualifications:

M.D. Ophthalmology at the prestigious All India Institute of Medical Sciences, New Delhi.
